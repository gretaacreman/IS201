  4. Adele
    5. Who is Adele?
    Adele is an English singer-songwriter who rose to fame in 2006 after her music posted on Myspace caught the eye of record lables. Her first album titled, "19", was released in 2008 and quickly became number one on the British album charts. A year later, she won Grammy awards for best new artist and best female pop vocal performance. Adele has come out with three additional albums all being named after her age; "21", "25", and "30". "30", released in 2021, became the best-selling album of that year and sold 4.68 million copies.

  5. Mac Miller
    6. Who is Mac Miller?
    Mac Miller is an American rapper and singer-songwriter from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Miller signed a record deal in 2010 with Rostrum Records and released his breakthrough mixtapes "K.I.D.S" and "Best Day Ever". He had released a total of six studio albums (all of which debuted among the Billboard top five) in which four of these albums were released before his high school graduation. Miller also founded a record label. Sadly, in 2018, Mac Miller passed away due to a drug overdose, which he had struggled with for a majority of his life. His music still heavily impacts music and talent today.
